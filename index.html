Our Aim

It is a known fact that there is tremendous talent in Indian children as in children anywhere in the world. All they need is the right Training Program to 'Fine tune' their inherent skills. Our Aim is therefore to fine-tune the talent of our students through rigourous training that will not only make them a great player but also a better human being by gaining the social values at our camps. We do this by following a simple process as under...



  • Intensive Training



  • Inter School Competitions



  • National Tournaments


  • Winner!!

Who we are...

Indus Global Sports Foundation has been formed as a trust aiming at providing professional training to their student to achieve their goals in sports. Coaching will be provided by well qualified coaches with all modern sports facilities in Football , Chess , martial arts . Our Academy is formed with the objective of providing physical and mental growth of youngsters and development of their calibre through various sports activities as well as other necessities to help, develop and nurture the talents of interested children. The academy uses the most updated skills and knowledge to teach our learners. Our progression of camps and programs, Led by our expert staff, are geared toward youth looking for a sport introduction or those who want to refine and master their sport-specific skills.
